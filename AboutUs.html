Brigid





Brigid is a junior at Melrose High School. Her interest in technology began when she joined her 5th grade First Lego League team. Brigid was first introduced to Algorithmic Bias over the Summer of 2020 while attending Boston University's AI4ALL program. Since then, Brigid has worked with Dara on a presentation to her school's STEM Pathway organization about how Algorithmic Bias occurs in the justice system.

Dara





Dara is a senior at Melrose High School. She attended Boston University's AI4ALL program with Brigid last summer. Since then she has stayed involved with the organization by leading a "signifigant interest group" of 7 other students from across the country in discussions about algorithmic bias. This group is creating a report on the state of federal legislation on facial recognition in the US. They will also create a website.
