Students Against Algorithmic Bias was built upon the idea that everyone deserves the opportunity to learn about how algorithmic bias is impacting their lives. We have provided reliable resources on Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Algorithmic Bias as well as opportunities to amplify the voices of those who are impacted by these technologies. We believe that Artificial Intelligence is an effective tool when used correctly. We want to work to make a difference in technology and help fix the disparities found in AI today.
